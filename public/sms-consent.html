SMS Messaging Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2026 · Last updated: June 1, 2026

Quick summary: We only text you about your own appointments. You can stop messages at any time by replying STOP. Standard message and data rates may apply.

1. Who Sends These Messages

SMS messages are sent by Lumière Beauty & Nail Studio, 218 Maple Court Suite 5, Riverside, CA. Our sending number is the one that appears in your message thread.

2. What You Consent To

By providing your mobile phone number when creating an account or making a booking, you consent to receive transactional SMS messages from us related to your appointments. These messages may include:

  • Booking confirmation texts when you make a new appointment.
  • Appointment reminders sent the day before your visit.
  • Cancellation or rescheduling notifications if your appointment changes.
  • Waitlist notifications if a time slot you requested becomes available.

We do not send marketing, promotional, or advertising SMS messages. You will not receive texts about sales, discounts, or offers.

3. Message Frequency

Message frequency depends on your booking activity. You may receive up to 3 messages per appointment (confirmation, reminder, follow-up). Most customers receive 1–6 messages per month.

4. How to Opt Out

You can stop receiving SMS messages at any time:

  • Reply STOP to any message we send. You will receive one final confirmation text and no further messages.
  • Email us at hello@lumierestudio.com to request removal.
  • Call us at (555) 240-1180 and ask to be removed from SMS notifications.

If you opt out, you will no longer receive appointment reminders. You can still manage your bookings online at Manage My Booking.

5. How to Get Help

Reply HELP to any message to receive our contact information, or reach us at:

  • Email: hello@lumierestudio.com
  • Phone: (555) 240-1180

6. Message & Data Rates

Standard message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan. Lumière Beauty & Nail Studio does not charge for SMS messages. Contact your carrier for details about your plan.

7. Supported Carriers

SMS messages are delivered through major US carriers including AT&T, T-Mobile, Verizon, and others. Carrier support is not guaranteed for all carriers or MVNO services. Message delivery is subject to carrier network availability.

8. Privacy

Your phone number is used solely to send appointment-related SMS messages as described above. We do not share your phone number with third parties for marketing purposes. For more information, see our Privacy Policy.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this SMS policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects any changes. Continued receipt of messages (i.e., not opting out) constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
