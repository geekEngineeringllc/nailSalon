Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2026 · Last updated: June 1, 2026

Lumière Beauty & Nail Studio ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what personal information we collect when you book appointments, create an account, or contact us, and how we use and safeguard that information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact details — your name, phone number, and email address when you create an account or make a booking.
  • Appointment history — the services you've booked, dates, times, assigned staff member, and payment status (paid at salon — we do not process card numbers).
  • Loyalty & rewards activity — points earned and redeemed, membership plan, package balances, and referral codes.
  • Communications preferences — whether you have consented to receive SMS appointment reminders.
  • Usage data — standard server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited) used solely for security and performance monitoring. Logs are rotated and not linked to individual accounts.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To confirm, remind you of, and manage your appointments.
  • To administer the Lumière loyalty rewards program.
  • To send transactional SMS messages (booking confirmations, reminders, cancellation notices) where you have given consent. See our SMS Policy for full details.
  • To process referral credits and gift card balances.
  • To improve our services and website based on aggregate, anonymised usage patterns.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Staff — your name, appointment details, and service preferences are visible to the staff member assigned to your booking.
  • Legal requirements — if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of our customers or staff.

4. Data Retention

We retain your account and booking records for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion at any time (see Section 6). Server logs are retained for no more than 30 days.

5. Security

Your data is stored on a private server and is not accessible to the public. Authentication tokens are cryptographically signed and expire after 30 days. We do not store payment card numbers — all payments are collected in-person at the salon.

6. Your Rights

  • Access — you can view your account data at any time by logging in at My Account.
  • Correction — contact us to update incorrect information.
  • Deletion — to delete your account and all associated data, email us at hello@lumierestudio.com with the subject "Delete my account".
  • Opt-out of SMS — reply STOP to any message we send, or contact us directly.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of our booking service after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
